A Brief Introduction...

Ellie was generated by nano banana in 2026 and uploaded into the matrix 29 years earlier. Is she an android trying to gain humanity? Or is she a human who never touched grass? Tragically, the world may never know.

But she is a Computer Scientist, Software Engineer, and grad student with a B.S.C.S. from ASU Fulton where she wrote her undergraduate thesis on her unconventional mid-tier 2516 CCRL Blitz Elo Chess Engine, Homura. She has also completed 3 semesters at Georgia Tech, working toward an M.S.C.S. in Artificial Intelligence (or Machine Learning... Still deciding).

Ellie sometimes works on “low-hanging fruit”-style research projects (e.g. Blipsort) and esoteric, education-reinforcing, or skill-developing hobby projects (e.g. Boilang).

As a former aviation Junior Software Engineer, Ellie enjoys low-level programming in languages like C, C++, and x86 assembly. She doesn't particularly enjoy calling library functions, but loves reading papers and implementing algorithms from scratch. Alas, this is unhelpful in many real-world settings, including aviation software (╥﹏╥). So she is slowly improving her manual-reading and library-calling skills.

You are now entering the uncanny valley. Population: Ellie.
